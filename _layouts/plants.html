Plants and Soil
  The purpose of this section is to research and display the scalability of technologies that are well-known to niche organic growers but remain nascent in commercial horticulture. This dichotomory is a "known-known" among academic researchers and enthusiasts alike; there are numerous examples of products that have successfully made the leap with many more remaining unadopted commercially

  Lastly, I'd be remiss if I didn't thank my late father for dragging me to Aalsmeer and Floriade in 2002, seeding the interest for this project. I also owe a major thank you to my organic farming friends in Kentucky and Chicago who have graciously answered soil and microbiome questions without hesitation.
